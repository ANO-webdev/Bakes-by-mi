Our Story

Crafted with Love in the Heart of Abuja

Bakes by Mi was born from a passion for creating beautiful, delicious food that turns ordinary moments into lasting memories. Based in Wuye, Abuja, we specialise in custom celebration cakes, decadent dessert cups, freshly baked pastries, and savory dishes — all made from scratch with the finest ingredients.

Every item that leaves our kitchen is a labour of love — designed to your taste, crafted to perfection. Whether it's a show-stopping birthday cake, a celebratory spread, or a delicious dessert cup, we are here to make your moment unforgettable. Sweet. Savory. Perfect. That's Mi.

Choose & Order
