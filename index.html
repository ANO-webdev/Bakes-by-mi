Our Story

Baked with Passion in the Heart of Abuja

Doolilis Bakery was born from a deep love for creating beautiful, delicious confections that turn ordinary moments into lasting memories. Based in Diamond City Estate, Lugbe, Abuja, we specialise in custom celebration cakes, pastries, and cake mixes made with premium ingredients.

Every cake that leaves our kitchen is a labour of love — designed to your taste, crafted to perfection, and delivered fresh. Whether it's your child's first birthday or your dream wedding, we are here to make it unforgettable.
